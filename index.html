Project Overview

Welcome to this ePortfolio, which chronicles my academic journey investigating the role and effectiveness of dashboards within the dynamic Fintech sector. This project showcases the progression from initial theoretical exploration via a literature review, through the practicalities of designing a research study in the proposal stage, to a final reflection on the skills and knowledge acquired. The central theme is understanding how data visualisation tools, specifically dashboards, influence critical processes like investment decision-making, risk management, and overall productivity in financial technology contexts. It highlights the development of critical analysis, research design, and academic communication skills throughout the module.

Contents

Navigate through the different stages of this research project:

  • Literature Review – Delves into existing academic research on dashboard use, performance management, and business intelligence in Fintech, showcasing the development of critical engagement with scholarly sources.
  • Research Proposal – Outlines a plan for investigating dashboard effectiveness, including the research aims, questions, methodology (mixed-methods), ethical considerations, and a detailed project timeline.
  • Reflection – Provides a detailed critical overview of the entire learning process, covering key takeaways, responses to tutor feedback, artefacts produced, experiences working autonomously, emotional journey, and commitment to academic integrity.
